– About the Author

Eldo Frezza is an internationally published author whose work explores medicine, power, corruption, and hidden worlds through suspenseful and research-driven storytelling.

Dr. Eldo Frezza is a world-renowned surgeon whose literary career is built on the bedrock of real-world medical experience. For over two decades, he has navigated the high-stakes environment of the operating room, where decisions are measured in heartbeats.

The Death of Venice – Book Summary

When the evil wizard Tribuka sets his sights on Venice, his dark ambition is held in check by a wager with Merlin and Morgana Le Fey. As long as the people of Venice care for their city, Tribuka cannot claim it. If they turn away, however, Venice will be swallowed by a fearsome tsunami.

For centuries, Morgana safeguarded Venice by raising paladins who defended the city and inspired its citizens in moments of danger. But a new threat has emerged — apathy. Pollution, corruption, and crime now grip the city, and only one person still cares enough to fight for it.

Marco, a philosophy student, must rediscover the legacy of Venice’s paladins and decide whether he is willing to give his heart to a city that seems to have forgotten itself. His love borders on obsession — but is one man’s devotion enough to overcome indifference, political failure, and the looming shadow of Tribuka? And will Genevieve help him find balance before it’s too late?

Covid and Other Demons – Book Summary

Dr. Savino’s medical expertise draws the attention of the FBI as the world faces the threat of a global biological catastrophe. What begins as a medical emergency quickly escalates into an international conspiracy.

From the outbreak’s suspected origins in Wuhan, China, to the shadowy black markets of Germany and Oman, Dr. Savino embarks on a perilous mission to intercept a stolen antidote and stop a virulent virus unleashed by a powerful underground organization.

Echoing the intensity of the Jason Bourne series and the medical-detective edge of Tess Gerritsen’s The Surgeon, this thriller introduces a unique hero — part surgeon, part covert agent. Fans of Michael Palmer and Robin Cook will find this story fast-paced, intelligent, and impossible to put down.
